Open Security Controls Assessment Language (OSCAL) is a NIST-led initiative developed in collaboration with industry to modernize and automate the processes of security and compliance. It provides open, machine-readable formats available in XML, JSON, and YAML that streamline control-based risk assessments.

By digitizing and automating traditional, paper-based compliance documentation, OSCAL dramatically reduces audit durations from months to minutes, minimizes human error, and accelerates compliance with evolving regulations.
